Case Study Spotlight

From Data to Decision

When the dashboard went offline — and everything stopped

At The GTC Group, I built a Power BI dashboard that connected our CRM, email marketing, social media and website data into a single weekly leadership view. Before it existed, performance data lived in five different platforms — nobody could see the full picture.

The real proof of its value came when I went on leave. Without access to the dashboard, the team had to retrieve data manually from the CRM. The weekly leadership meeting ran significantly longer and the CEO immediately flagged that the dashboard needed to be on Power BI Service so every team member could access it independently.

That moment confirmed what good data work should do — it should become so essential to how an organisation operates that its absence is immediately felt.

Single source of truth for weekly meetings
